HELLO!

 

I’m Andrea, and I’m a writer and digital content producer (and sometime jazz singer) living in little ol’ England, in big ol’ London.

Specialising in Fun Stuff™,  I write articles, scripts, jokes, spoof news stories, listicles, sketches and parody songs. I digitally produce similar things, including videos, pictures and content for social media accounts.

Before going freelance, I was the Comedy Editor of The Huffington Post UK for three years. I’ve also been a cinema manager, a stand-up comedy promoter and an interactive TV producer, though not all at the same time. My hobbies include taking photos, wearing big scarves and daydreaming about owning a dog.
