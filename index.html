Dr. He Wang (BEng, PhD),
Associate Professor

I am an Associate Professor (my faculty page) at the Visualisation and Computer Graphics group, in the School of Computing, University of Leeds, UK. I am also a Turing Fellow, an Academic Advisor at the Commonwealth Scholarship Council, the Director of High-Performance Graphics and Game Engineering, and an academic lead of Centre for Immersive Technology. My current research interest is mainly in computer graphics, vision and machine learning and applications. Previously I was a Senior Research Associate at Disney Research Los Angeles. I received my PhD and did a post-doc in the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh. Before my PhD, I worked in industry for 4 years after graduating from the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, China.
